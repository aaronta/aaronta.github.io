I am the Assistant Director of the Biomedical Imaging Center at the Beckman Institute, University of Illinois Urbana‑Champaign. My research spans magnetic resonance imaging methods development and applications including Magnetic Resonance Elastography (MRE), advanced MR spectroscopic and metabolic imaging, and multimodal neuroimaging for clinical and cognitive neuroscience.

Additionally, I am a Clinical Research Fellow in the [Carle Neuroscience Institute][cni] at [Carle Health][carle], Adjunct Clinical Research Professor in the Department of Veterinary Clinical Medicine, [College of Veterinary Medicine][vetmed], and Clinical Assistant Professor in the Departments of [Clinical Sciences][clinical] and [Neurology and Neurosurgery][neuro], [Carle Illinois College of Medicine][cimed].

For publications and a complete list of works see my [ORCiD](https://orcid.org/0000-0003-0440-3416) and [Google Scholar profile](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=I9J4kcAAAAAJ&hl=en).
