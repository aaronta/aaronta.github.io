Welcome

Welcome — I am the Assistant Director of the Biomedical Imaging Center at the Beckman Institute, University of Illinois Urbana‑Champaign. My work focuses on MR methods development — including Magnetic Resonance Elastography (MRE), MR spectroscopic imaging, and multimodal neuroimaging — and on translating methods to clinical and cognitive neuroscience applications.

Please explore the site to find my research, news, and CV. You can also find my publications on ORCiD and Google Scholar.
